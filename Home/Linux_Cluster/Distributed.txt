What is a Distributed Systems Cluster?
A distributed systems cluster a a group of machines that are virtually or geographically separated,
and that work together to provide the same service or application to clients.	[[https://www.informit.com/articles/article.aspx?p=25748&seqNum=4#:~:text=A%20distributed%20systems%20cluster%20is,service%20or%20application%20to%20clients.&text=It%20is%20defined%20as%20a,seamless%20access%20to%20a%20service.|[ref 1]]]
