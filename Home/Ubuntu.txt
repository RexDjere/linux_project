What is Ubuntu?
Ubuntu is a Linux distribution based on Debian, and composed mostly of free and open-source software.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu|[ref 1]]]

What does Ubuntu mean?	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu|[ref 1]]]
Ubuntu is named after the Nguni philosophy of ubuntu, 
which Canonical indicates means "humanity to others"
with a connotation of "I am what I am because of who we all are".
