What is Fedora?
Fedora is  +Linux_distribution developed by the community-supported +Fedora_Project which is sponsored primarily by +Red_Hat, a subsidiary of +IBM , with additioanl support from other companies.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fedora_(operating_system)|[ref 1]\]]

What does Fedora contain?
Fedora contains software distributed under various +free_and_open_source licenses and aims to be on the leading edge of free technologies.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fedora_(operating_system)|[ref 1]]]

For what software is Fedora an upstream source?
Fedora is the +upstream source for the commercial [[+Red_Hat_Enterprise_Linux]] distribution and for [[+CentOS]] .	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fedora_(operating_system)|[ref 1]]]

From was software was Fedora forked?
Fedora is a fork of Red Hat Linux since RHL was discontinued in 2003.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fedora_(operating_system)|[ref 1]]]
