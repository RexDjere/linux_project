Describe Linux device drivers.	[[https://lwn.net/Kernel/LDD3/|[ref 1]]]
* special role in the Linux kernel.
* are distinct "black boxes".
* make a particular piece of hardware respond to well-defined interna; programming interface.
* hide the details of how the device works.
* map standardized calls to device-specific operations.
